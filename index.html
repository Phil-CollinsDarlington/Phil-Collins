Who I Am

About Me

Passionate about technology, driven by impact.

Based in Kampala, Uganda

Building Digital Experiences That Matter

I'm Phil Collins Darlington, an aspiring IT professional, web developer, and technology entrepreneur based in the heart of Kampala, Uganda. My journey into technology started with a simple curiosity "How do websites work?". That question sparked a passion that has since grown into a full-blown mission.

I specialize in designing and developing clean, fast, and purposeful websites for individuals, startups, and small businesses. Whether you're a local entrepreneur needing your first website or an established business looking to upgrade your digital presence, I'm here to turn your vision into reality.

Beyond web development, I provide hands-on IT support, software installation, computer setup, and technical consultation, bridging the gap between complex technology and everyday users right here in Uganda.

My long-term vision is ambitious: to build a technology company that serves Africa's growing digital economy, creating jobs, solving real problems, and putting Uganda on the global tech map.

  • Fluent in English & Luganda
